Second Chance Adoption Centre is a shelter to rescue & rehabilitate pets who have been abandoned or abused by their owners or discarded by breeders.

Total no. of Dogs Adopted: 3000

Want to Help?
You too can grant the gift of a second chance to these wonderful souls who deserve a better life. To adopt a pet, fill out the form below. Or if you just want to contribute to our centre, consider making a donation. You are also welcome to pay us a visit during our open hours.

All of the dogs at the HEADS & TAILS Second Chance Adoption Center have been rescued from accidents, abuse, and negligence. It is very important to note that adopting a dog is a long-term commitment depending on the age of the dog. Many dogs are abandoned and given up due to relocation of the family, promotions in jobs, changes in family structures (expecting a baby, marriage, senior parents coming to stay with the family), and allergies. Kindly please ensure that you and your family have extensively talked through and agreed on the decision to adopt before making a decision.
