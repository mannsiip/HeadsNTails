PASSIVE ADOPTION

Always wanted to adopt a pet but unable to do so because of family, space, or time constraints? With our passive adoption program, you can sponsor one of our rescues—be it a dog, cat, horse, cow, goat, or any animal you connect with—for as little as Rs. 3000 per month. Your sponsorship covers their monthly expenses for food and medical care while they continue residing at our shelter. You are welcome to visit them and spend quality time whenever you like!
